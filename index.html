Don't just capture the moment - create it.

PicZag is an iPhone App that lets friends take and share photos in a fun unique way. Every photo becomes the backdrop for friends to create their own masterpieces.

With PicZag, your pictures become canvases for collaborative editing. Snap a shot, send to friends, and let the creativity flow.

Enhance your pictures

Make every minute one that counts with stickers, collages, and your own unique edits. Work, school, and the bus have never looked this good!

Edit together

Don't let your friends get the last punch. Use all of our editing features to turn the picture you received into the picture you want!

Bring more friends in the fun

On PicZag, you can collaborate with as many friends as you want. Create groups for easy sharing with one, two, three or more!

Share it with the world

The world is your stage! Share your pictures to the PicZag Hall of Fame or to your other social media outlets, and let everyone experience the fun.

Explore

Inspiration or entertainment. Enjoy the funniest, most creative and most outrageous pictures created by users for users in the Hall of Fame.
